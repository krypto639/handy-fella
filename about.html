About

Handyfella is operated by Brent Whyte and covers the Nelson/Tasman region. Brent has a varied range of experience covering painting, renovation of spaces and property maintenance. His previous work experience has given him a good work ethic, good time management and quality workmanship and presentation. Brent has the can-do attitude to get your job done.

Ready to give Handy Fella a go?

Just give us a call at 021 108 0950 or drop us an email at brentwhyte@handyfella.co.nz and we'll get right onto it.

Handy information that can help us with your job is a list of the services required, urgency of the job completion and accessibility to the site for parking and equipment.
